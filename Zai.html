How to Join the Treasure Hunt

  1. Download the FestifyXR app or visit our web platform
  2. Register for the Saturnalia Treasure Hunt challenge
  3. Scan QR codes located at various points across the festival
  4. Complete challenges at each location to earn clues
  5. Collect all clues to discover the final treasure location
  6. Redeem your treasure for exclusive festival rewards
